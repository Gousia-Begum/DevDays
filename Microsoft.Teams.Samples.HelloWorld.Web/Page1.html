Microsoft 365 Platform: Building Modern Productivity Applications
Time : 2019-05-21 09:30 - 10:15
Speaker : Daniel Canning /Senior Product Marketing Manager, Office Ecosystem
Keyur Patel /Program Manager, Office Developer Platform Team
Dan Stevenson /Principal PM Manager, Microsoft 
                    Microsoft Teams is the fastest growing business application in Microsoft's history, used by over 500,000 companies around the world. Teams is about more than just chat or files -- it's really an operating system for applications and a powerful front-end for many Office 365 services. For application developers, Teams provides a rich set of extensibility points built on a secure platform. It's easy to build everything from bots up to full-screen experiences, plus leverage deep integrations with the Office/Microsoft platform from PowerApps, Flow, and SharePoint, plus flexible provisioning capabilities via the Microsoft Graph.
                    In this keynote session, we will demonstrate the power and breadth of the Office and Teams platforms for developers and how to enable your users to take action across all their services in an integrated way. See how organizations worldwide accelerate application engagement, improve user efficiency and focus, and create modern workflows across a wide range of systems and services.
